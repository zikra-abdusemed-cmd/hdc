Harar Dev Center is the first community-driven learning hub in Harar, dedicated to technology, creativity, and innovation. We run hands-on programs in web & mobile development, AI, and essential digital skills

Who We Are

Harar Dev Center was founded by passionate young innovators who believe in the power of technology and education to transform lives. We are a community-driven hub that provides access to digital skills, mentorship, and innovation opportunities for students, graduates, and young professionals in Harar.

Our Mission

To create a culture of learning, creativity, and innovation in Harar, enabling young people to compete globally while solving local challenges.

Our Vision

A Harar where every young mind has the tools, knowledge, and support to innovate, build, and succeed.
